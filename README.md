## Key

- mark: `m`
- yank: `y`

---

- undo: `u`
  - line: `U`
- redo: `Ctrl + r`

---

- top of file: `gg`
- end of file: `G` 
- screen top: `H`
- screen bottom: `L`
- beginning of line: `0`
  - soft: `^` (shift + 6)
- end of line: `$` (shift + 4)

---

- insert: `i`
  - at bol: `I`
- append: `a`
  - at eol: `A`
- open below: `o`
  - above: `O` 

---

- delete: `d`
  - to eol: `D`
- replace: `r`
  - mode: `R`
- subst char: `s`
  - line: `S`
- change: `c`
  - to eol: `C`
- paste after: `p`
  - before: `P`
